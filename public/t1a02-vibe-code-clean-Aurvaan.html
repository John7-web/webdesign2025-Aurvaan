The Arctic Fox

The arctic fox ($Vulpes~lagopus$) is a small fox native to the Arctic regions of the Northern Hemisphere. Its fur changes color with the seasons, from a thick, white coat in winter to a brown or gray coat in summer, providing excellent camouflage. They are well-adapted to cold environments, with a rounded body shape to minimize heat loss and thick fur on their paws.
