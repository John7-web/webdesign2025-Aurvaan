The Majestic Red Panda

The red panda ($Ailurus~fulgens$) is a small mammal native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. They are slightly larger than a domestic cat and are known for their reddish-brown fur, long shaggy tail, and waddling gait. Despite their name, they are not closely related to the giant panda. The red panda is listed as an endangered species, primarily due to habitat loss and fragmentation.
